AP PGCET 2026 — Key Details

The Andhra Pradesh Post Graduate Common Entrance Test (AP PGCET) is a state-level entrance exam conducted by Andhra University, Visakhapatnam, on behalf of APSCHE. It is the single gateway for students seeking admission into MA, M.Com, M.Sc, MCJ, M.Lib.Sc, M.Ed, M.P.Ed and other postgraduate programs in specialized universities and their affiliated colleges across Andhra Pradesh.

Conducting BodyAndhra University, Visakhapatnam
LevelPostgraduate (State Level)

Eligibility Criteria

General Eligibility

  • ✔ Bachelor's Degree in relevant discipline from a recognized university.
  • ✔ Minimum 50% aggregate marks (45% for SC/ST/BC candidates).
  • ✔ Final year degree students are also eligible to apply.

Course Specifics

  • ✔ M.Sc: B.Sc with relevant subjects.
  • ✔ M.Com: B.Com or relevant degree.
  • ✔ M.A: B.A with relevant subject as one of the core subjects.
