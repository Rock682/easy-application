Understanding AP PGCET 2026

The Andhra Pradesh Post Graduate Common Entrance Test (AP PGCET) is a state-level entrance exam conducted by Andhra University, Visakhapatnam, on behalf of APSCHE. It is the single gateway for students seeking admission into MA, M.Com, M.Sc, MCJ, M.Lib.Sc, M.Ed, M.P.Ed and other postgraduate programs in specialized universities and their affiliated colleges across Andhra Pradesh.

Conducting Body

Andhra University, Visakhapatnam

Level

Postgraduate (State Level)

Application Process

Fee Payment

Visit the official cets.apsche.ap.gov.in portal and pay the registration fee online.

2

Fill Application

Enter your qualifying degree details, personal info, and select your exam subject and center.

3

Upload & Submit

Upload your photo and signature. Submit the form and take a printout for reference.

Eligibility Criteria

school General Eligibility

  • ✔ Bachelor's Degree in relevant discipline from a recognized university.
  • ✔ Minimum 50% aggregate marks (45% for SC/ST/BC candidates).
  • ✔ Final year degree students are also eligible to apply.

category Course Specifics

  • ✔ M.Sc: B.Sc with relevant subjects.
  • ✔ M.Com: B.Com or relevant degree.
  • ✔ M.A: B.A with relevant subject as one of the core subjects.

Syllabus Details

The syllabus for AP PGCET 2026 is based on the undergraduate curriculum of Andhra Pradesh universities. It is divided into three categories:

Category I Arts, Humanities, Social Sciences
Category II Commerce & Education
Category III Science Subjects

*The detailed subject-wise syllabus can be downloaded from the official website.
