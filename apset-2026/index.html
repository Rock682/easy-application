Eligibility Criteria

APSET 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must fulfil the following eligibility conditions to appear for APSET 2026.

Educational Qualifications:

  • Master's Degree or equivalent from a UGC-recognized university.
  • General/EWS Candidates: Minimum 55% marks (without rounding off).
  • BC/SC/ST/PWD/Transgender Candidates: Minimum 50% marks (without rounding off).
  • Candidates who have appeared for their final year Master's examination and are awaiting results are also eligible (must pass within two years).
  • Ph.D. holders who completed their Master's Level examination by September 19, 1991, are eligible for a 5% relaxation in marks (55% to 50%).

Age Limit:

There is no upper age limit for applying for APSET.

Students must meticulously review these criteria to confirm their eligibility.

How to Apply

APSET 2026 Application Process

The application process for APSET 2026 is entirely online. Candidates must visit the official website to register and submit their applications.

How to Apply Online:

  1. Visit Registration Portal: Go to the Official APSET Registration Portal.
  2. Application Fee Payment: Click on "Application Fee Payment" and complete the transaction using Credit Card, Debit Card, or Net Banking.
  3. Check Payment Status: Once paid, use your Reference ID to "Know Your Payment Status" and ensure success.
  4. Fill Application Form: Click on "Fill Application Form" to enter personal, academic, and category details.
  5. Document Upload: Upload scanned photograph and signature as per size specifications.
  6. Submit & Print: Carefully review the data, submit the form, and "Print Application Form" for future reference.

Ensuring all necessary documents and information are ready before starting is crucial to avoid last-minute issues.

Exam Pattern & Syllabus

APSET 2026 Examination Pattern

The APSET examination consists of two papers held in a single day. All questions are objective type (Multiple Choice Questions).

Paper Subject Questions Marks Duration
Paper-I General Paper (Teaching & Research Aptitude) 50 100 1 Hour
Paper-II Subject Paper (Selected Subject) 100 200 2 Hours

There is no negative marking for incorrect answers. Paper-I is bilingual (English & Telugu for most subjects), while Paper-II is in English (except for language subjects).

Candidates should familiarize themselves with the detailed syllabus and exam pattern for effective preparation, understanding question types, marking scheme, and time duration.

Paper-I is common for all candidates and tests Teaching/Research Aptitude.

  • Teaching Aptitude: Nature, objectives, characteristics, and basic requirements.
  • Research Aptitude: Meaning, characteristics, and types.
  • Communication: Nature, characteristics, types, barriers, and effective classroom communication.
  • Reasoning (including Mathematical): Number series, letter series, codes, relationships, and classification.
  • Logical Reasoning: Structure of arguments, deductive and inductive reasoning.
  • Data Interpretation: Sources, acquisition, and interpretation of data.
  • Information and Communication Technology (ICT): General abbreviations and terminology, basics of internet, e-mailing.

Master Paper-I

Paper-I is often the deciding factor. Practice reasoning and DI daily to save time during the exam.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the APSET 2026 examination?
APSET (Andhra Pradesh State Eligibility Test) is a state-level exam conducted to determine the eligibility of candidates for the post of Assistant Professor or Lecturer in degree colleges and universities across Andhra Pradesh.
Who conducts APSET 2026?
APSET 2026 is conducted by Andhra University, Visakhapatnam, on behalf of the Government of Andhra Pradesh.
What is the eligibility for APSET 2026?
Candidates must have a Master's degree with at least 55% marks (50% for reserved categories) from a UGC-recognized university. There is no upper age limit.
When is the APSET 2026 exam?
The APSET 2026 examination is scheduled to be conducted on March 28 and 29, 2026.
What are the application fees for APSET 2026?
The application fees are: ₹1,600 for General candidates, ₹1,300 for BC/EWS, and ₹900 for SC/ST/PH/Transgender candidates.
