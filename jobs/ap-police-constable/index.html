AP Police Constable 2026 Recruitment

The State Level Police Recruitment Board Andhra Pradesh (SLPRB AP) conducts recruitment for Constable, Warder, Fireman and Sub-Inspector posts in the Andhra Pradesh Police Department. AP Police Constable 2026 is a major state-level opportunity for young candidates seeking a career in law enforcement. Selected constables serve in district police forces, maintain public order, assist in crime prevention and undergo rigorous training at police academies before field deployment.

Eligibility

  • Nationality: Indian citizen.
  • Domicile: Must be a resident of Andhra Pradesh and satisfy local status requirements.
  • Educational Qualification: 10th pass (SSC) for Constable posts; Intermediate (12th) for Sub-Inspector (SI) posts.
  • Age Limit: 18–22 years for Constable (as per latest notification). Age relaxation for SC/ST/BC/PwBD as per AP government rules.
  • Physical Standards: Minimum height 167 cm (male) / 152 cm (female); chest 81–86 cm (male, expanded); weight proportionate to height.
  • Physical Efficiency Test: 1600 m run, long jump, high jump and other events as per SLPRB standards.

Advertisement

Selection Process

  • Preliminary Written Examination: Objective test covering Arithmetic, Reasoning, General Studies and General English/Telugu.
  • Physical Measurement Test (PMT) & Physical Efficiency Test (PET): Qualifying physical standards and endurance tests.
  • Final Written Examination: Detailed subject test for candidates who qualify PMT/PET.
  • Medical Examination: Eye sight (6/6 or 6/9), general health and fitness assessment.
  • Document Verification and final merit list based on written exam marks.

How to Apply

  1. Go to slprbap.gov.in when the Constable recruitment notification is active.
  2. Click 'Apply Online' and register with valid email and mobile number.
  3. Fill the application with personal, educational, category and address details.
  4. Upload photograph, signature and any required certificates in specified dimensions.
  5. Pay application fee through online payment gateway.
  6. Submit and print the application form; download hall ticket for preliminary exam when available.
