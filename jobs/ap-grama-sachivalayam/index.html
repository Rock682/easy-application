Andhra Pradesh Grama Sachivalayam (Village Secretariat) system delivers governance services at the village and ward level through a network of secretariats across the state. AP Grama Sachivalayam recruitment 2026 fills posts such as Village/Ward Secretary, Panchayat Secretary, ANM, Welfare Assistant, Surveyor, Lineman and other multi-department cluster posts. These jobs offer government pay scales, local posting and direct community service, making them highly sought after among intermediate and graduate candidates in Andhra Pradesh.

Eligibility

  • Nationality: Indian citizen.
  • Domicile: Must be a resident of Andhra Pradesh; local status in the mandal/ward applied for is typically required.
  • Educational Qualification: Intermediate (12th) for most posts; Degree required for Village/Ward Secretary and certain technical posts.
  • Age Limit: 18–42 years (may vary by notification). Relaxation for SC/ST/BC/PwBD/ex-servicemen as per AP rules.
  • Other Requirements: Knowledge of Telugu; computer basics preferred for secretariat posts.
  • Disqualification: Candidates dismissed from government service or with criminal convictions are ineligible.

Advertisement

Selection Process

  • Written Examination: Objective-type test covering General Studies, Mental Ability, English/Telugu and subject-specific knowledge.
  • Merit List: Prepared district-wise and post-wise based on exam marks and reservation rules.
  • Document Verification: Educational certificates, local status, category and age proof verified.
  • Posting: Selected candidates posted to Village/Ward Secretariats in their allotted mandal or ward.
  • Training: Induction training provided on government schemes, digital governance and citizen services.

How to Apply

  1. Open gramawardsachivalayam.ap.gov.in and check for active recruitment notification.
  2. Click 'Apply Online' and complete registration with personal and contact details.
  3. Select the post and mandal/ward preferences as per your local status eligibility.
  4. Upload required documents — photo, signature, educational certificates.
  5. Pay the application fee through the online payment system.
  6. Submit and save the application number; download hall ticket when exam dates are announced.
