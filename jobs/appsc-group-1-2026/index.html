APPSC Group 1 Services 2026

Andhra Pradesh Public Service Commission (APPSC) Group 1 Services examination recruits candidates for the highest executive posts in the state government. APPSC Group 1 2026 includes posts such as Deputy Collector (Deputy Executive Officer), Deputy Superintendent of Police (DSP), Commercial Tax Officer (CTO), District Fire Officer, District Panchayat Officer and other Class I and Class II executive positions. This is the premier state civil services exam for Andhra Pradesh graduates seeking leadership roles in district administration, police, revenue and allied departments.

Eligibility

  • Nationality: Indian citizen.
  • Domicile: Must satisfy local/non-local status requirements of Andhra Pradesh as per Presidential Order.
  • Educational Qualification: Bachelor's degree from a recognized university. Specific posts may require additional qualifications.
  • Age Limit: 18–42 years (varies by post). Relaxation: SC/ST/BC — 5 years; PwBD — 10 years as per AP rules.
  • Physical Standards: DSP and Fire Officer posts require minimum height, chest and physical fitness standards.

Advertisement

Selection Process

  • Screening Test: Objective-type preliminary examination to shortlist candidates for Main Examination (number of candidates shortlisted is typically 50 times the vacancies).
  • Main Examination: Descriptive written examination covering General English, Telugu, General Essay, and three papers on History & Culture, Constitution & Governance, and Economy & Development.
  • Interview (Oral Test): 75 marks assessing candidate's suitability for executive posts.
  • Final merit based on Main Examination marks + Interview marks.
  • Medical examination and document verification before appointment.

How to Apply

  1. Visit psc.ap.gov.in and register on the APPSC One-Time Profile Registration (OTPR) portal.
  2. Log in and apply for Group 1 Services notification when published.
  3. Fill personal, educational, category and local status details; upload photo and signature.
  4. Pay application fee online as prescribed in the notification.
  5. Submit application and download acknowledgement; note your hall ticket number.
  6. Download hall ticket for Screening Test from APPSC portal when released.
