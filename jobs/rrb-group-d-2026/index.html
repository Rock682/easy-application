RRB Group D 2026 — Level 1 Railway Posts

Verified guide — eligibility, selection process, and official apply link. Always confirm dates on the government portal before applying.
Category: Central Govt Jobs Updated: June 2026

RRB Group D recruitment fills Level 1 posts in Indian Railways for candidates with a 10th pass or ITI qualification. RRB Group D 2026 includes posts such as Track Maintainer Grade IV, Helper/Assistant in various engineering departments, Pointsman, Porter and other operational roles. These positions offer job security, railway allowances and career progression under the National Career Service framework for central government employees.

Eligibility

  • Nationality: Indian citizen.
  • Educational Qualification: 10th pass (Matriculation) from recognized board OR ITI certificate from NCVT/SCVT in relevant trade.
  • Age Limit: 18–33 years. Relaxation: OBC — 3 years; SC/ST — 5 years; PwBD — 10 years.
  • Medical Standards: Must qualify medical fitness category as prescribed (e.g., B1 for Track Maintainer, C1 for certain helper posts).
  • Physical Efficiency: Candidates must pass Physical Efficiency Test (PET) — lifting weights, running specified distance within time limit.

Advertisement

Selection Process

  • Computer Based Test (CBT): Mathematics, General Intelligence & Reasoning and General Science & General Awareness (90 minutes, 100 questions).
  • Physical Efficiency Test (PET): Qualifying in nature — male candidates run 1000 m in 4 min 15 sec; female candidates in 5 min 40 sec; lifting and carrying 35 kg (male) / 20 kg (female) for 100 metres.
  • Document Verification: Verification of educational, category and other certificates.
  • Medical Examination: Final fitness assessment as per railway medical standards.
  • Final merit list prepared based on CBT marks (PET is qualifying only).

How to Apply

  1. Go to rrbcdg.gov.in and select your regional Railway Recruitment Board.
  2. Find the Group D / Level 1 recruitment notification and click 'Apply Online'.
  3. Register, fill personal and educational details, and select post preferences.
  4. Upload photo, signature and pay the application fee online.
  5. Submit the application and note your registration number.
  6. Download admit card when CBT dates are announced on the RRB portal.
