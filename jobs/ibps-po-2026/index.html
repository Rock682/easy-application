IBPS PO 2026 — Probationary Officer Recruitment

Verified guide — eligibility, selection process, and official apply link. Always confirm dates on the government portal before applying.
Category: Central Govt Jobs Updated: June 2026

📅 Last updated: June 18, 2026

IBPS conducts the Common Recruitment Process (CRP) for Probationary Officer / Management Trainee posts in participating public sector banks. IBPS PO 2026 is a national-level banking examination that selects graduate candidates for officer cadre positions offering attractive salary (Scale I), allowances and career growth in banks such as Bank of Baroda, Canara Bank, Punjab National Bank and others. Applications are submitted exclusively through ibps.in during the notification window.

Eligibility

  • Nationality: Indian citizen.
  • Educational Qualification: Graduation degree in any discipline from a university recognized by the Government of India.
  • Age Limit: 20–30 years. Relaxation: OBC — 3 years; SC/ST — 5 years; PwBD — 10 years; Ex-servicemen and others as per IBPS rules.
  • Computer Literacy: Operating and working knowledge of computer systems is essential.
  • Language: Proficiency in the official language of the state/UT where applying is required.

Advertisement

Selection Process

  • Preliminary Examination: English Language, Quantitative Aptitude and Reasoning Ability (100 questions, 60 minutes). Qualifying in nature for Mains.
  • Main Examination: Reasoning & Computer Aptitude, General/Economy/Banking Awareness, English Language and Data Analysis & Interpretation plus Descriptive English (letter and essay writing).
  • Interview: Conducted by participating banks; assesses personality, communication and banking awareness.
  • Provisional Allotment: Based on combined Mains + Interview marks, candidates are allotted to participating banks.
  • Training: Selected POs undergo intensive training before branch posting.

How to Apply

  1. Open ibps.in and click on CRP PO/MT notification under 'CRP' section.
  2. Click 'New Registration' and provide basic details to get Provisional Registration Number and Password.
  3. Upload photograph, signature, left thumb impression and handwritten declaration as per IBPS specifications.
  4. Fill educational details, category, state applying for and pay application fee (₹850 General/OBC; ₹175 SC/ST/PwBD).
  5. Submit application and download the call letter when Prelims admit card is released.
  6. After qualifying each stage, download respective call letters from the IBPS candidate portal.
