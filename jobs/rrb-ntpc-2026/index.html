RRB NTPC 2026 — Non-Technical Popular Categories

Verified guide — eligibility, selection process, and official apply link. Always confirm dates on the government portal before applying.

RRB NTPC (Non-Technical Popular Categories) recruitment fills graduate and intermediate-level posts across Indian Railways. RRB NTPC 2026 covers posts including Junior Clerk cum Typist, Accounts Clerk cum Typist, Junior Time Keeper, Commercial cum Ticket Clerk, Traffic Assistant, Goods Guard, Senior Commercial cum Ticket Clerk, Senior Clerk cum Typist, Junior Account Assistant cum Typist, Senior Time Keeper, Commercial Apprentice (CA) and Station Master (SM). Applications are submitted online through regional RRB websites listed on rrbcdg.gov.in.

Eligibility

  • Nationality: Indian citizen.
  • Educational Qualification: Varies by post — Graduate degree for SM, CA, Goods Guard and most clerk posts; 12th pass for Junior Clerk, Junior Time Keeper and certain other posts.
  • Age Limit: 18–33 years for most posts (specific post-wise age limits apply). SC/ST — 5 years; OBC — 3 years; PwBD — 10 years relaxation.
  • Medical Standards: Candidates must meet railway medical fitness standards (A2, B1, B2, C1 categories) as applicable to the post.
  • Other: Candidates with pending criminal cases or dismissed from government service are not eligible.

Selection Process

  • Stage 1 CBT: Common exam for all candidates — Mathematics, General Intelligence & Reasoning and General Awareness (120 minutes).
  • Stage 2 CBT: Post-specific exam for shortlisted candidates — Mathematics, General Intelligence & Reasoning, General Awareness and Basic Science & Engineering.
  • Typing Skill Test (TST): For Clerk and Account Assistant posts — minimum 30 wpm in English or 25 wpm in Hindi.
  • Computer Based Aptitude Test (CBAT): Mandatory for Station Master and Traffic Assistant posts.
  • Document Verification & Medical Examination: Final stage before appointment.

How to Apply

  1. Visit rrbcdg.gov.in and identify your regional RRB (based on domicile or choice).
  2. Click the active NTPC 2026 recruitment link on the respective RRB website.
  3. Register with Aadhaar-linked mobile number and email; complete the application form with educational and post preference details.
  4. Upload photograph, signature and required certificates in prescribed format.
  5. Pay application fee (₹500 for General; ₹250 for SC/ST/Ex-servicemen/PwBD/EWS — ₹400 refundable on appearing for CBT).
  6. Submit and print the application; monitor RRB website for exam city, admit card and result.
