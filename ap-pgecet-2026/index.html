The AP Post Graduate Engineering Common Entrance Test (AP PGECET) 2026 is the primary pathway for engineering and pharmacy graduates to secure admission into M.Tech, M.Pharm, and Pharm.D (PB) courses in Andhra Pradesh universities. Get ready for the next step in your academic career.

AP PGECET 2026 Highlights

Exam Name: AP PGECET

Level: State Level Postgraduate

Courses: M.Tech, M.Pharm, Pharm.D (PB)

Mode: Online (CBT)

Important Dates (Official)

how_to_reg Apply Online Officially
  • Notification Release: February 4, 2026
  • Online Application Starts: February 6, 2026
  • Last Date (No Late Fee): March 6, 2026
  • Late Fee Submission: March 11 (₹1000) to March 23 (₹10000)
  • Correction Window: March 24-25, 2026
  • Download Hall Tickets: April 15, 2026
  • Exam Dates: April 28, 29 and 30, 2026
  • Results Release: May 14, 2026

Eligibility Criteria

  • Nationality: Indian National, AP/Telangana Domicile.
  • Qualification: B.E./B.Tech/B.Pharm or equivalent with at least 50% marks (45% for reserved categories).
  • GATE/GPAT: Candidates with valid GATE/GPAT scores are exempt from the entrance test and given priority in admission.

How to Apply

  1. Visit the APSCHE PGECET official website.
  2. Pay the registration fee online.
  3. Fill in personal, academic, and GATE/GPAT details (if applicable).
  4. Upload photo and signature.
  5. Submit and print the application form.

Exam Pattern

The exam is conducted online for 2 hours.

Details Description
Total Questions 120 MCQs
Total Marks 120 Marks
Marking Scheme 1 Mark per correct answer. No Negative Marking.

Syllabus Breakdown

GATE/GPAT Qualified Candidates

Candidates with valid GATE/GPAT scores are exempt from the entrance test. They must apply directly for counseling.

AP PGECET Exam (For Non-GATE/GPAT)

The syllabus is based on the undergraduate curriculum of the respective branch (Civil, Mech, CSE, ECE, EEE, Pharmacy, etc.). The exam is of 120 marks.

Counseling Procedure

  • Phase 1 (GATE/GPAT): Separate notification is released. Priority in admission.
  • Phase 2 (AP PGECET Ranks): Web counseling for remaining seats.
  • Certificates Required: Rank Card, Hall Ticket, Degree Certificates, Memo of Marks, Caste Certificate.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GATE mandatory for AP PGECET? expand_more
No. GATE qualified candidates get exemption and priority, but non-GATE candidates can write AP PGECET to secure admission.
What is the exam fee? expand_more
Approximately ₹1200 for OC, ₹900 for BC, and ₹700 for SC/ST candidates (estimated based on previous years).
