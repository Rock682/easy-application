Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility

Candidates should have passed graduation (B.A./B.Sc./B.Com/B.C.A./B.Tech) with at least 50% marks. Age limit: Minimum 19 years.

Exam Pattern & Syllabus

Exam Pattern

The test comprises 150 objective questions (150 Marks). Duration: 2 Hours.
