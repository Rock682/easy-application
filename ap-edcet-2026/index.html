AP EdCET 2026: Official Notification & Dates

Home AP EdCET 2026

AP EdCET (Education Common Entrance Test) 2026 is conducted for admission into 2-Year B.Ed (Bachelor of Education) Regular courses in Andhra Pradesh.

Exam Highlights

Exam Name AP EdCET
Purpose B.Ed Admission
Conducting Body Andhra University
Mode Online

Important Dates (Official)

  • Official Notification: February 3, 2026
  • Online Application Starts: February 11, 2026
  • Last Date (No Late Fee): April 2026
  • AP EdCET 2026 Exam Date: May 4, 2026

Eligibility

Candidates should have passed graduation (B.A./B.Sc./B.Com/B.C.A./B.Tech) with at least 50% marks. Age limit: Minimum 19 years.

Exam Pattern

The test comprises 150 objective questions (150 Marks). Duration: 2 Hours.

Subject Marks
General English 25
General Knowledge 15
Teaching Aptitude 10
Methodology (Subject) 100

Syllabus Breakdown

Part A: General English (25 Marks)

Reading Comprehension, Correction of Sentences, Prepositions, Tenses, Vocabulary.

Part B: General Knowledge & Teaching Aptitude (25 Marks)

Current Affairs, Educational Issues, Teaching Profession attributes.

Part C: Methodology (100 Marks)

Mathematics, Physical Sciences, Biological Sciences, Social Studies, English (Choose one based on your degree).

Counseling Process

  • Registration: Register on the official specialized portal.
  • Verification: Upload certificates (Degree, Inter, SSC, Caste, Income).
  • Web Options: Select colleges offering B.Ed in your methodology.
  • Allotment: Download order and join the college.
