Common Questions (FAQ)

How do I apply for AP CET exams?
You need to visit the official AP SCHE website for the respective exam. We provide the direct links and step-by-step guidance on each exam page.
What documents are required for application?
Generally, you need your educational certificates (SSC/Inter), Aadhaar card, password size photo, and signature. Specific requirements vary by exam.
Can I correct my application after submission?
Yes, most exams provide a "Correction Window" for a few days after the application deadline. Check our Key Dates section for details.
