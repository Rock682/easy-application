AP ICET 2026 — Key Details

The Andhra Pradesh Integrated Common Entrance Test (AP ICET) is a state-level entrance exam conducted by Andhra University, Visakhapatnam, on behalf of APSCHE. It is the mandatory gateway for students seeking admission into MBA (Master of Business Administration) and MCA (Master of Computer Applications) programs in specialized universities and their affiliated colleges across Andhra Pradesh.

Conducting BodyAndhra University, Visakhapatnam
LevelPostgraduate (State Level)

Eligibility Criteria

For MBA Admission

  • ✔ Bachelor's Degree (any discipline) from a recognized university.
  • ✔ Minimum 3 years duration of undergraduate study.
  • ✔ At least 50% marks (45% for reserved categories).

For MCA Admission

  • ✔ Bachelor's Degree (BCA/B.Sc/B.Com/B.A) with Mathematics at Inter or Degree level.
  • ✔ Minimum 50% aggregate marks in graduation.
  • ✔ Candidates in their final year can also apply.

Preparation Tips

Success in AP ICET requires a balanced approach between logical reasoning and analytical skills. The exam is divided into three main sections: Analytical Ability, Mathematical Ability, and Communication Ability.

Time Management

Focus on solving Analytical and Data Sufficiency questions quickly to save time for Math.

Syllabus Clarity

Master 10th-level Arithmetic, Algebra, and Geometry for the Mathematical section.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check my AP ICET results? expand_more
You can check your results on the official APSCHE website once announced. You will need your hall ticket number and date of birth to download the rank card.
What is the validity of the AP ICET 2026 score? expand_more
The AP ICET score is valid for admission into the MBA/MCA programs strictly for the current academic year only (2026-27).
