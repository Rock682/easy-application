Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

EasyApplications ("we", "us", "our") operates easyapplications.in, an informational website for government job notifications and entrance exam guides. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our site.

Information We Collect

  • Usage data: Pages visited, browser type, device type, approximate location (via Google Analytics).
  • Contact information: Only if you email us at help@easyapplications.in.
  • Advertising data: Google AdSense may collect data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.

Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Website analytics (Google Analytics)
  • Advertising (Google AdSense)
  • Remembering your cookie consent preference

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that some features may not work correctly if cookies are disabled.

Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on EasyApplications. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Alternatively, visit aboutads.info to opt out of third-party vendor cookies for personalized ads.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites. Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to easyapplications.in and/or other sites on the Internet.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends without identifying individual visitors. Learn more at Google Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Links

Our pages contain links to official government websites (SSC, RRB, UPSC, APPSC, APSCHE, etc.) and other external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites.

Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for general audiences including students and job seekers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold. Contact us at help@easyapplications.in for any privacy-related requests.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

Privacy questions or concerns: help@easyapplications.in
