AP ECET 2026: Official Notification, Exam Dates & Eligibility

Home AP ECET 2026

The Andhra Pradesh Engineering Common Entrance Test (AP ECET) 2026 is the gateway for lateral entry admission into the second year of B.Tech/B.Pharmacy courses for Diploma holders and B.Sc (Mathematics) degree holders. It offers a crucial opportunity for students to upgrade their qualifications.

AP ECET 2026 Important Dates

  • Official Notification: February 3, 2026
  • Online Application Starts: March 2026 (Expected)
  • Last Date (Without Late Fee): April 2026 (Expected)
  • AP ECET 2026 Exam: May 2026 (Tentative)

These dates are subject to change based on the official release by APSCHE.

AP ECET 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must meet the following eligibility requirements to apply:

Nationality & Domicile:

  • Indian Nationals.
  • Must belong to Andhra Pradesh / Telangana and satisfy local status requirements.

Educational Qualifications:

  • Diploma Holders: Diploma in Engineering/Technology/Pharmacy from the State Board of Technical Education of AP/Telangana or any recognized institution. Minimum 45% marks (40% for reserved categories).
  • B.Sc (Mathematics): 3-year B.Sc Degree with Mathematics as one of the subjects from a recognized University. Minimum 45% marks (40% for reserved categories).
  • Candidates appearing for final year Diploma/Degree exams in 2026 are also eligible.

How to Apply for AP ECET 2026

The application process is online-only.

  1. Visit Official Website: Go to the APSCHE ECET portal.
  2. Fee Payment: Pay the registration fee online.
  3. Fill Application: Enter Diploma/B.Sc details, personal information, and choice of exam center.
  4. Upload Images: Upload your photo and signature.
  5. Print Application: Submit carefully and take a printout.

For B.Sc (Mathematics) students, the pattern differs slightly with 100 marks for Maths, 50 for Analytical Ability, and 50 for Communicative English.

For Pharmacy students, subjects include Pharmaceutics, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Pharmacognosy, and Pharmacology (50 marks each).

AP ECET 2026 Detailed Syllabus (Stream-Wise)

The AP ECET syllabus is based on the Diploma curriculum for Engineering/Pharmacy and B.Sc (Maths) degree syllabus.

General Subjects (Common)

  • Mathematics: Matrices, Trigonometry, Calculus, DIfferential Equations.
  • Physics: Units & Dimensions, Elements of Vectors, Kinematics, Heat & Thermodynamics.
  • Chemistry: Atomic Structure, Chemical Bonding, Solutions, Acids & Bases, Electrochemistry.

Engineering Streams (100 Marks)

  • CSE: Digital Logic, Computer Organization, Data Structures, C Programming, DBMS, OS.
  • ECE: Electronic Devices, Circuit Theory, Digital Electronics, Communication Systems.
  • EEE: Basic Electrical Engg, DC Machines, AC Circuits, Power Systems.
  • Civil: Strength of Materials, Surveying, Hydraulics, Structure Analysis.
  • Mechanical: Workshop Technology, Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics.

AP ECET 2026 Counseling Process

Post-result declaration, the APSCHE conducts Web Counseling for seat allotment.

  1. Registration: Candidates must register online at the counseling portal and pay the processing fee.
  2. Certificate Verification: Visit designated Help Line Centers (HLCs) for document verification.
  3. Web Options Entry: Choose preferred colleges and courses online.
  4. Seat Allotment: Seats are allocated based on rank, reservation, and choices.
  5. Reporting: Download allotment order and report to the allotted college.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the rank validity for AP ECET 2026?
The rank obtained in AP ECET 2026 is valid only for admission into courses for the academic year 2026-2027.
Can students with backlogs apply for AP ECET?
Yes, students appearing for final exams can apply, but they must clear all backlogs and pass the qualifying examination by the time of admission.
Is there any negative marking in AP ECET?
No, there is no negative marking for incorrect answers.
