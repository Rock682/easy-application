AP POLYCET 2026: Official Notification (Expected) & Dates

Home AP POLYCET 2026

AP POLYCET (Polytechnic Common Entrance Test) 2026 is a state-level entrance examination conducted by the State Board of Technical Education and Training (SBTET), Andhra Pradesh. It is the gateway for admission into Diploma courses in Engineering, Non-Engineering, and Technology offered at Polytechnics across the state.

Exam Highlights

Exam Name AP POLYCET
Purpose Diploma Admission
Conducting Body SBTET, Andhra Pradesh
Mode Offline (OMR)

Important Dates (Expected)

  • Notification Release: March 2026
  • Online Application Starts: March 2026
  • Last Date to Apply: April 2026
  • Exam Date: April 2026 (Last Week)

Eligibility Criteria

  • Nationality: Indian National, AP Domicile.
  • Qualification: Passed SSC (Class 10) or equivalent with min 35% marks.
  • Subjects: Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry are mandatory.
  • Candidates appearing for SSC exams in 2026 are also eligible.

Exam Pattern

The exam is offline (OMR based) with a duration of 2 hours. No negative marking.

Subject Questions Marks
Mathematics 50 50
Physics 40 40
Chemistry 30 30
Total 120 120

Syllabus Breakdown

The syllabus is based on the Class 10 (SSC) curriculum prescribed by BSEAP.

Mathematics

Number System, Algebra, Trigonometry, Geometry, Mensuration, Statistics, Probability.

Physics

Heat, Optics (Reflection & Refraction), Electricity, Magnetism, Electronics.

Chemistry

Atomic Structure, Chemical Bonding, Acids & Bases, Metallurgy, Carbon Compounds.
