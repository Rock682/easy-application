Eligibility Criteria

AP EAPCET 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must meet the following eligibility requirements to apply:

Nationality & Domicile:

  • Indian Nationals / PIO / OCI card holders.
  • Must belong to Andhra Pradesh / Telangana and satisfy local status requirements.

Educational Qualifications:

  • Engineering: 10+2 with Physics, Mathematics, and Chemistry. Minimum 45% marks (40% for reserved categories).
  • Agriculture & Pharmacy: 10+2 with Biology, Physics, and Chemistry.
  • Candidates appearing for final year exams in 2026 are also eligible.

Age Limit:

Candidates should have completed 16 years of age as of 31st December 2026. There is no upper age limit for Engineering.

How to Apply

How to Apply for AP EAPCET 2026

Applications will be accepted online only through the official APSCHE website.

Apply on Official Website →
  1. Fee Payment: Visit the official website cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET and pay the registration fee.
  2. Check Payment Status: Verify your payment status using your hall ticket number and mobile number.
  3. Fill Application: Complete the application form with personal and academic details.
  4. Print Application: Submit the form and take a printout for future reference.
