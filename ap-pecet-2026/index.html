AP PECET (Physical Education Common Entrance Test) 2026 is a state-level entrance exam for admission into U.G.D.P.Ed (2 Years) and B.P.Ed (2 Years) courses.

Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility Criteria
  • B.P.Ed: Bachelor's Degree in any discipline. (Min. 19 years)
  • U.G.D.P.Ed: Intermediate (10+2) pass. (Min. 16 years)

Exam Pattern & Syllabus

Test Pattern

The entrance test consists of two parts (Total 500 Marks):

Component Details Marks
Physical Efficiency Test 100m, Shot put, Long Jump, etc. 400
Skill Test Game of choice (Cricket, Football, etc.) 100
