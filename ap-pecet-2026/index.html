AP PECET (Physical Education Common Entrance Test) 2026 is a state-level entrance exam for admission into U.G.D.P.Ed (2 Years) and B.P.Ed (2 Years) courses.

Exam Highlights

Exam Name AP PECET
Conducted By Acharya Nagarjuna Univ.
Mode Physical Efficiency Tests
Level State Level

Important Dates (Official)

  • Official Notification: February 13, 2026
  • Online Registration Starts: February 13, 2026
  • Last Date (Without Late Fee): April 30, 2026
  • Last Date (Late Fee ₹1000): May 15, 2026
  • Last Date (Late Fee ₹2000): May 25, 2026
  • Correction Window: May 24-26, 2026
  • Hall Ticket Download: May 28, 2026 Onwards
  • Physical Efficiency Tests: June 3 - June 8, 2026 (6:00 AM)
  • Results Declaration: One week after tests

Official Link: cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/PECET

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.P.Ed: Bachelor's Degree in any discipline. (Min. 19 years)
  • U.G.D.P.Ed: Intermediate (10+2) pass. (Min. 16 years)

Test Pattern

The entrance test consists of two parts (Total 500 Marks):

Component Details Marks
Physical Efficiency Test 100m, Shot put, Long Jump, etc. 400
Skill Test Game of choice (Cricket, Football, etc.) 100

Physical Efficiency Test (Standard)

Compulsory Events (Men & Women)

  • Run: 100 meters / 800 meters.
  • Jumps: Long Jump / High Jump.
  • Throwing: Shot put (6kg Men / 4kg Women).

Counseling Procedure

  • Rank Card: Download rank card after results.
  • Registration: Register for web counseling.
  • Physical Verification: NCC/CAP/Sports certificates verified at HLCs.
  • Seat Allotment: Based on Physical Efficiency Test + Skill Test marks.
